Controller_Module.determine_optimal_sample_rate(joystick=None)[source]¶

Poll the joystick slowly (beginning at 1 sample per second) and monitor the packet stream for missed packets, indicating that the sample rate is too slow to avoid missing packets. Missed packets will translate to a lost information about the joystick state. As missed packets are registered, increase the sample rate until the target reliability is reached.
